Chicken Recipe

The following is a recipe for Chicken.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup chili sauce
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 5 chicken thighs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Iri medium saucepan over medium heat, blend cider vinegar and chili sauce.
  3. Heat oil in a medium sillet over medium heat.
