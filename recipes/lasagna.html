Lasagna Recipe

The following is a recipy for lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1(16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1(16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese
    • <1i>pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 min.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. In a medium bowl combine mozzarellam cheddar and ricotta; stir well.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
